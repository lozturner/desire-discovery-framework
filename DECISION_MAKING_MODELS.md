# Decision-Making Models for Business
## Frameworks to Help People Discover What They Want

---

## 1. Values-Based Decision Making

### Core Principle
Decisions aligned with personal values lead to higher satisfaction

### Process
1. Identify 5-7 core values
2. Evaluate options against values
3. Choose highest alignment

### Business Application
- Strategic planning
- Hiring decisions
- Product development

### Key Benefit
Reduces decision fatigue and post-decision regret

---

## 2. Outcome Visualization Model

### Technique
Mentally simulate future outcomes before deciding

### Steps
1. Define ideal outcome
2. Visualize typical day
3. Test emotional response
4. Identify gaps

### Business Use
- Customer journey mapping
- Product vision
- Organizational change

### Advantage
Reveals implicit assumptions and uncovers hidden desires

---

## 3. Constraint Removal Framework

### Method
Temporarily suspend real-world constraints to identify true desires

### Questions
- "If money wasn't an issue..."
- "If failure was impossible..."
- "If time was unlimited..."

### Business Context
- Innovation workshops
- Blue-sky thinking sessions
- Competitive differentiation

### Outcome
Separates means (constraints) from ends (actual goals)

---

## 4. Peak Experience Analysis

### Approach
Analyze past moments of peak satisfaction to extract patterns

### Process
1. Identify 3-5 peak moments
2. Dissect what made them special
3. Find common themes

### Business Application
- Employee engagement
- Customer satisfaction research
- Brand positioning

### Insight
Past peak experiences reveal core values and preferences
